Breakout Blast!

Lives: 0

Score: 0

How To Play:

Use the left and right arrow keys (or the buttons onscreen) to move the paddle horizontally across the bottom of the screen. The ball bounces off the walls, the paddle, and the bricks. Hit the ball against the bricks to destroy them; once a block reaches 0, it is destroyed. If the ball falls below the paddle, you lose a life. Destroy all the bricks to advance to the next level. Hitting a block gains 1 point, while destroying a block adds 10 points.
