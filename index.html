Breakout Blast!

Lives: 0

Score: 0

How To Play:

Use the left and right arrow keys (or swipe on a touchscreen) to move the paddle horizontally across the bottom of the screen. The ball bounces off the walls, the paddle, and the bricks. Hit the ball against the bricks to destroy them; once a block reaches 0, it is destroyed. If the ball falls below the paddle, you lose a life. Destroy all the bricks to advance to the next level (or screen). Hitting a block gains 1 point, while destroying a block adds 10 points.
